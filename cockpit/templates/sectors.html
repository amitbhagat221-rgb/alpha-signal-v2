{# Methodology card — explains "composite score" #}
How to read the score
Avg composite = mean of stock-level final_score across stocks in the sector, where each stock's final_score is a cap-tier-weighted blend of validated factor signals (consensus · piotroski · accruals · earnings yield · book-to-price · promoter · forensic + macro and regulatory tilts). Higher is better. Breadth = % of stocks in the sector with composite ≥ 0.55. Macro comes from macro_sector_signals (RBI / sector index / regulatory tilts mapped per sector).

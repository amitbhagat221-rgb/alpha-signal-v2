How to read the score
Avg composite = market-cap-weighted average of stock-level final_score within the industry: Σ(score × mcap) / Σ(mcap). Each stock's final_score is itself a cap-tier-weighted blend of validated factor signals. So a ₹10L cr leader dominates the industry score, and 50 micro-caps can't drag a sector down on their own. Breadth = % of stocks with composite ≥ 0.55 (equal-weighted — catches "is the bench deep?" alongside the cap-weighted leader signal). Industry granularity uses our 25-bucket taxonomy (e.g. Consumer Discretionary splits into Automobiles, Auto Components, Hospitality, Retail, E-Commerce, Consumer Durables).

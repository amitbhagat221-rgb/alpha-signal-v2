★ Divergent — pick appears in this variant's top {{ n_per_tier }} but in neither of the other two variants. The most informative cell — these are picks whose ranking is driven by the weight-scheme choice rather than the underlying signals.
How to read this:
Production was tuned by hand from a single t-stat per signal. Max Return + Max Sharpe were derived from the PIT IC backtest and surface two factors that production missed: pt_upside (t = 7-9 across tiers) and eps_growth (t = 3-5).

Max Return weights factors by |t-stat| — favors factors with biggest absolute IC, even if volatile.
Max Sharpe weights factors by ICIR (mean IC ÷ vol of IC) — favors consistency. Lower expected return per trade, lower variance.

Both variants gate at 40% eligible coverage (vs production's 60%) because they concentrate ~40% of SMALL weight on analyst-dependent signals; the looser gate keeps stocks without analyst coverage in the SMALL universe.

Not yet persisted — these variants are CLI / cockpit only. To persist them to daily_picks, the schema needs a variant column and the screener cron needs to run all three. Defer until you decide whether to actually switch live picks to one of the new schemes.

Per-Factor — Data Health & Validation
Data Health = 65% coverage · 25% freshness · 10% PIT-ready, all cadence-aware. Coverage is graded against the right denominator for the factor's nature:
  broad = % of universe · sparse = "is data flowing?" (insider/bulk/sentiment only cover stocks with the event) · sector / composite / data-depth = per-stock coverage doesn't apply.
Rule of thumb: A/B/C = trust it · D = look here · F = broken or unbuilt.
Validation = KEEP (|t|≥2.5) · WEAK (|t|≥1.5) · DROP (|t|<1.5) · INSUFFICIENT (n<12 periods — preliminary, await deeper backtest) · NONE (no backtest yet) — independent of data health.
